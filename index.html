Our Mission

To create accessible works of art in beautiful script that inspire the mind and the artist within.

Our Story

We began in 1972 - a wild band of smelly hippies in a garage and a VW van with a vision to share beauty through the written word. Since then, we've expanded from just our basic, personalized, calligraphic canvases and offer a variety of different products - wall-hangings, wall decals, baseball caps, mousepads and several more items to decorate and personalize your home, office and life with.
